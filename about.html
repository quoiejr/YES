Who We Are

Youth For Empowerment And Solidarity (YES) is a community-driven organization dedicated to amplifying the voices of young people in refugee and host communities. We believe that by providing digital literacy, creative skills, and leadership training, we can foster a generation of resilient leaders ready to advocate for their rights and futures.

Mission

Empowering youth through solidarity and creative action.

Vision

To Build a Generation of Empowered, skilled and Globally competitive younf creative-digital innovators who drives social change, economic growth and national development.

Mission Statement

To Identify young talents, equip them with practical digital and creative skills and connect them to mentorship, platform and opportunities that turn creativity into sustainabl careers.

Our Story

Youth for Empowerment and Solidarity (YES) was founded on the belief that young people aren't just the leaders of tomorrow—they are the change-makers of today. By rooted ourselves in unity and branching out through action, we bridge the gap between passion and impact.
